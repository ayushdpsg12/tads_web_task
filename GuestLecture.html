Lt. Col. Romil Bhartawal



An alumnus of IIT Kharagpur, he has successfully led a 20-member expedition to scale Mt. Everest. Amongst other adventure activities, he has done 200, 300, 400, 600 km cycling brevets in one year to achieve the title of a super Randonneur. Not only was he a podium finisher in an Ironman event but also a certified white water rafting course expert.


stepping forward to face adventure, he has done bungee jumping, duathlons, paramotor, parasailing, paragliding and para trooping too.
